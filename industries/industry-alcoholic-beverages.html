Alcoholic Beverages: Top Sellers Hold Steady, But Big Swings for Assorted Beer

Lager, White Still, and Red Still remain the top 3 subcategories by sales rank, with no changes in their positions from the prior period.

Notably, Assorted Beer saw a substantial sales increase of 19.9%, while Sour experienced the largest decline at -9.4%. Red Still and Bourbon/Whiskey/Scotch each improved one rank, while Malt, Premixed Cocktails, Rum, Brandy/Cognac, and Wheat each dropped one position.

Source: Circana, Liquid AI™, August, 2025

Data Source: Point of Sale (POS) Data
