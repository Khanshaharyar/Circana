History in the Making

Circana is built on the strong foundation of IRI and NPD, two of the world’s leading market information companies for decades.

Our Roots

Built on the combined strength of IRI and NPD

Decades of market intelligence come together to deliver a more complete view of the consumer and the market.

IRI

John Malec and Gerry Eskin founded IRI in 1979 to track consumers’ behavior and connect it to the products they bought at the supermarket. Leveraging first-to-market technology, IRI built one of the industry’s largest repositories of purchase, media, social, causal, and loyalty data—fueling growth for more than 5,000 companies worldwide.

npd

NPD was founded in 1966 by Henry Brenner, who pioneered a system to record consumers’ buying habits through purchase diaries. Today, more than 2,000 companies rely on NPD to measure, predict, and improve performance with data, industry expertise, and prescriptive analytics that prepare businesses for what’s next.

We Are

Circana

Circana™ is a leader in providing technology, AI, and data to fast-moving consumer packaged goods companies, manufacturers, and retailers seeking to optimize their businesses. Our predictive analytics and technology empower clients to measure market share, uncover the consumer behaviors driving it, and accelerate growth with confidence. Circana's Liquid Data™ platform is powered by an expansive, high-quality data set and intelligent algorithms refined across six decades of domain expertise.
