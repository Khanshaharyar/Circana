Unify+

The Unify+ solution helps you visualize trends, work together, and build custom reports - all in one place. Your team can adjust data in real time and automate repetitive tasks to focus on the insights that matter.

Intelligence Suite

Circana's Intelligence Suite™ solution helps you integrate, harmonize, and automate data workflows across your business. Built for scalability, this cloud-based, flexible solution connects various data sources, powers real-time automation, and delivers insights to support faster and more confident decision-making.

Liquid AI

Developed to answer your most pressing business questions, Liquid AI™ is a fusion of leading data assets, robust data science, and deep industry knowledge.

Liquid Data Collaborate

Circana's Liquid Data Collaborate™ solution helps you bring all your data together in one place - and puts it alongside our industry-wide view. You'll get a complete picture of the retail landscape across eight key market segments, from food and fashion to tech and health. Our technology platform gives you clear, actionable insights, all easily shareable with your most important vendors.

Liquid Data Go

Liquid Data Go® solution helps emerging and mid-sized CPG brands show their value with performance data from MULO+, the largest expansion of the total U.S. universe since 2011. With insights from POS data, panel behavior, e-commerce trends, and pricing across key retailers – including beauty, club, dollar, mass, grocery, and online pure play – brands can uncover market dynamics, track sales, and take action that drives growth.

Liquid Data Engage

Liquid Data Engage solution is a turnkey, collaborative solution built for retailers. It integrates your data assets and delivers curated insights with actionable recommendations to support customer-centric strategies. Powered by Circana's Liquid Data® technology, it provides consistent, scalable insights and a visually intuitive experience.

Liquid Mix

Liquid Mix™ is an AI-driven, self-serve tool that measures ROI, analyzes media spend impact, and finds optimization opportunities.
